Target Audience

The target audience is people of all ages looking for things that they can do in the city of orem whether they live in the city or they are just passing though. This may be as easy as somewhere to eat or maybe what is a good school for my kids to go to.

Persona 1

My first persona is going to be a father that just moved to the city and is looking to get settled in. He is looking to use the site to possibly find a place of employment as well as looking up the different schools for his kids. He is mostly going to be using the site on a computer as most of the things that he is looking for will be easier to find as well as he has all of his info and files already on the computer.

Persona 2

My second persona is a single person that is living in orem while going to school and looks up the website in hopes of finding fun activies to do as well as good places to eat. They will be using a cell phone to look up the site for the majority of the things that they are doing cause they are alway on the move and rarely are sitting at a commputer desk.

Scenarios

  • Where is a good place to get a hamburger on a buget?
  • What activies are going to be comming up in the near future?
  • What schools are around? What are their graduation rate?
  • Which organizations are highering? What is the base pay?
  • Where is the nearest book store? Do they sell manga?
