About Us

We are a small locally owned business specializing in organic home grown foods. We have been around for 7 years, and love seeing the smiles that we can bring to peoples faces. We are primarly located in Carlsbad, California, we do however have food trucks that travel as far north as Los Angeles and as far south as San Diego. Over the course of our years of service we have served over 700,000 customers and counting.

Carlsbad has been our home for many years and we love it here. Between the white sandy beaches and the mild climate that is here it is a great to visit at anytime of the year. Carlsbad is home to Legoland California that was opened in March of 1999, this was the first Legoland park that was build outside of Europe.
